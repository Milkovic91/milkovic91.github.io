Dice Game

Rules:

  • You will roll up to 10 dice, one at a time.
  • If you roll a 1 --- End Game!
  • If you roll 2 or 3, you can roll again!
  • If you roll 4, you'll lose 1 gold coin.
  • If you roll a 5, you win 5 gold coins and you get to roll again.
  • If you roll a 6, you win 6 gold coins and you get to roll again.
